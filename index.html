About TOGO

TOGO is a memecoin inspired by the legendary sled dog who braved the Alaskan wilderness to save lives. Just like the real Togo, this memecoin is all about resilience, loyalty, and the community pulling together.

🐾 5 Amazing Facts About Togo

  • 1. True Hero of the Serum Run: Togo ran over 260 miles — far more than any other dog — during the 1925 serum run to Nome.
  • 2. Underrated Legend: While Balto got most of the fame, Togo did the hardest and longest part of the journey.
  • 3. Born to Lead: Togo was originally considered too weak, but he proved himself and became Leonhard Seppala's top lead dog.
  • 4. Senior Strength: Togo was 12 years old (very old for a sled dog) during the serum run, yet still outperformed younger teams.
  • 5. Disney Immortalized Him: Togo’s true story was finally told in the 2019 Disney+ film “Togo,” earning him the recognition he deserved.
